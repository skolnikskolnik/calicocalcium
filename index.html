An application where you can get information on where to get tested for COVID-19 , including facility information, documentation needed, and test results in order to stop the spread of this pandemic.



What to bring:

  • Passport or driver license
  • Health Insurance, if you have it
  • Medical records
  • Face mask and a pen
  • Parental guardian, if under the age of 18
